Boki Brand Application

Date: Spring 2024

Skills: Motion Graphics, Brand Identity Application

Overview

"Boki is a pop-up shop and market service where Asian small business owners can showcase and sell their products to the public. Our vision is to provide consumers with the option to shop small Asian businesses, all in one place."

Boki is a concept brand designed by a classmate. My role in this project was to create brand applications using the brand guidelines created by my classmate. View the full project here.
