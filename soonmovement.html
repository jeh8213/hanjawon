Soon Movement Instagram Graphics

Date: Fall 2022 - Spring 2023

Role: Social Media Designer

Skills: Social Media Management, Visual Design, Brand Consistency

Overview

As the social media designer, my roles was to design visually appealing graphics for NYU's Soon Movement instagram. The graphics designed were for the weekly club meetings and occational events.

Tools

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Canva

Process

In order to maintain brand consistency as I took on this role, I looked into the overall theme and feel of the current instagram page, and collected this information for the brand identity:

Color tones: Neutral, soft, light tones

Style: Illustrative graphics, graphics relative to announcement

Typography: Handwritten/Script Fonts

Based on this information, I created visually consistent posts utilizing a combination of Adobe Illustrator and Canva.
